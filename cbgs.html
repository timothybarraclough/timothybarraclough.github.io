Cube Based Grid Sequencer

2012

Installation for OSX and Novation Launchpad. Users are able to control a series of 6 sequencers, with diffierent instruments, mapped to the faces of a cube. Programmed in a combination of Processing and ChucK, with sound design in Ableton Live.

flora

Paul F. C. Mathews and Timothy J. Barraclough

2015

the flora project was conceived as a suite of beautifully simple, CPU-effective audio effects for iOS Devices, reminiscent of stomp-box style effects.

A simple, consistent and intuitive interface is presented with just the right number of parameters to allow users to quickly dial in the perfect sound.

phlox phaser

stereo phase shifting audio effect.

cactus chorus

two-stage chorus effect with stereo crossover.

buttercup bitcrush

general purpose bit-crusher, sample-rate reduction & noise effect.

dahlia delay

tape style delay effect with filtered feedback and warble amount controls.

saffron saturator

filter drive, distortion and saturation effect.

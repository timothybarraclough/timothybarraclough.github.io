flora

Paul F. C. Mathews and Timothy J. Barraclough

2015

the flora project was conceived as a suite of beautifully simple, CPU-effective audio effects for iOS Devices, reminiscent of stomp-box style effects.

Simple interfacing principles were adhered to in order to present the user with only a handful of parameters for each effect in order to allow them more time to focus on the sound they want.

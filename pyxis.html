pyxis minor

2014

pyxis minor is a novel instrument / sequencer / synthesizer for Mac OS and iOS Devices.

Users play and record sequences in real time and are able to play with various synthesis parameters to create a tailored musical experience. Bright colours, sleek aesthetic and intuitive user interfacing combine to allow users of any age and prior musical experience and training to have a truly unique creative musical experience.

unique constellation themed sequencing

sequences of any length are recorded in real time, quantized to a global tempo control and played back instantly.

Synthesizer parameters

4 seperate instrument / sequences / synthesizers allow for individual parameter adjustments to the pitch, octave, waveform, envelope and gain.
